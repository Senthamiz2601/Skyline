Scopus Publication Guide

Understand Scopus indexing, journal quality, and publication process.

What is Scopus?

Scopus is a global abstract and citation database that indexes high-quality research journals, conferences, and articles.

  • Peer-reviewed journals only
  • Global research indexing system
  • Used for academic ranking

Why Scopus Matters

Scopus publications are considered high-impact and are required for:

  • PhD completion
  • University promotions
  • Research funding
  • Academic reputation
